How We Work

Four steps to a compliant,
thriving practice

A structured, proven methodology — from your first conversation to full ongoing support.

01

Strategic Assessment

We begin by understanding your practice, your goals and your current structure — mapping the optimal path forward for your specific situation.

02

Structure & Licensing

We establish your authorised representative status under our AFSL, set up your compliance framework and ensure you're structured to operate effectively from day one.

03

Investment Oversight

We provide the investment governance, model portfolios and review infrastructure to ensure your client recommendations meet AFSL obligations and best interest duties.

04

Compliance & Enablement

Ongoing supervision, CPD support, regulatory updates and business development resources — keeping your practice compliant, competitive and growing.

The Team

Meet Iconic Investors

AA
Antonio Albuquerque
Managing Director

Antonio Albuquerque brings 24 years of financial services experience and 16 years as a registered tax agent to Iconic Investors. A specialist in serving doctors and high-net-worth individuals, Antonio has spent his career helping fellow financial advisers build stronger practices — improving systems, technology, marketing, compliance and client communication. His deep understanding of both sides of the industry shaped Iconic Investors — a dealer group built by an adviser, for advisers.

TZ
Tony Zulli
Director, Iconic Investors

Tony Zulli brings more than 30 years of financial services experience to Iconic Investors, including senior roles at Citibank and a proven track record of building and running successful dealer groups across Australia. His deep institutional knowledge and industry relationships provide the strategic foundation that underpins everything Iconic does.
